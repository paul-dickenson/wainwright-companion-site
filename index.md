# The Wainwright Companion

**Track, navigate and complete all 214 Wainwright fells in the English Lake District.**

The Wainwright Companion is your pocket guidebook for bagging every fell in Alfred Wainwright's legendary Pictorial Guides. Whether you're ticking off your first summit or chasing the final few, the app keeps track of where you've been, guides you on the hill, and helps you decide where to go next.

## Features

- **Full checklist** of all 214 fells — search, filter by region, and track your progress
- **GPS navigation** with turn-by-turn route guidance and off-route alerts
- **Live weather forecasts** with summit-specific safety ratings so you know before you go
- **Smart suggestions** — "which fell should I bag this weekend?" based on weather, daylight, and your past progress
- **Detailed statistics** — elevation climbed, distance walked, completion by region, and more
- **Walk recording** — automatic GPS tracks with pace, ascent, and duration
- **Social features** — share completions, follow friends, and compare progress
- **iCloud sync** — your progress stays up to date across all your devices
